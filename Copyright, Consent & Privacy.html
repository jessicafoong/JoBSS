Copyright Statement

Copyright defines the ownership of the contents published in JoBSS. As an open-access journal, the copyright of the papers published in JoBSS belongs to the producers of the contents and authors of the papers.


Authors are allowed to share, publicize, reprint, or use the paper published in JoBSS freely if the ethics of publication and integrity of the contents are maintained. All the intellectual properties produced from the papers published belong to the authors.


The readers are allowed to share, reprint, or use the papers or contents taken from the papers published by JoBSS with clear and proper citation, where credits are given to the authors and the publisher (as stated in CC-BY).


Before the paper publication, the corresponding author must sign a Publishing Agreement Form (PAF) to give JoBSS permission to publish, distribute, and publicize the paper submitted by the authors. JoBSS retains exclusive publishing rights of the paper.

Consent and Privacy Statement

Human subjects of a study should be made known to the usage of the information taken from them. Consent from the subjects of a study, especially in clinical and medical research is essential in publishing related information. JoBSS requires all authors to declare the consent given by the subjects of study wherever necessary.


The privacy of human subjects should be kept to safeguard their rights. JoBSS therefore will not publish sensitive personal information such as national identification number or social security number under any circumstances.


All papers with personal information of human subjects will be rejected. Additionally, information that reflects discrimination on genders or ethnicity will also be rejected.

Personal Data Protection Statement

JoBSS recognizes the importance of protecting personal information of authors and institutes. Serving as an open-access journal that currently requires no publication fees, authors should not provide sensitive information such as national identification numbers or credit card numbers to JoBSS.


Identification of institutions might be requested whenever required. We will fully comply with personal data protection acts and will not tolerate any leaking of sensitive information.

Privacy Statement

All personal information furnished by authors is strictly used for scholarly publication purposes and will not be divulged to any third parties. JoBSS privacy policy is governed by Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010.
